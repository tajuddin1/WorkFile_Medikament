  • Doplnky stravy

    Výživové doplnky sú moderným prostriedkom prevencie viacerých druhov ochorení a podpory zdravia. Sú voľne dostupné bez lekárskeho predpisu, zvyčajne je možné kombinovať ich s bežne užívanými liekmi a pri dodržaní odporúčaného denného dávkovania sú bezpečné skoro pre každého.

  • Vitamins, minerals and immunity + vitality

    Our body needs these components in given amounts (which may vary depending on several factors, such as age, gender or associated diseases). The diet of modern man usually does not cover all the necessary vitamins and minerals that we should take in on a daily basis. Supplementing them is therefore important, the lack of vitamins and minerals can manifest itself on several levels, for example, reduced immunity, deteriorated quality of hair and nails, hair loss or neurological disorders. The assortment of vitamins nowadays is very wide, among the most frequently purchased are vitamins for immunity support (C, D, zinc ), vitamins for diabetics (a complex of vitamins that are most important for diabetes), vitamins serving as supportive treatment for heart diseases (with with added omega-3 fatty acids), thyroid diseases ( selenium , iodine ) or vitamins for liver detox (with added milk thistle). And which vitamins are suitable for supplementing energy? Look for those that contain various adaptogens that support the body during exercise, such as ginseng or maca .
